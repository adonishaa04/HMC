Our Well-Equipped Gymnasium

Maintain your fitness routine with our modern gymnasium, featuring a wide range of equipment for all your workout needs.

Gym Facilities & Equipment

Our gym is designed to cater to various fitness levels and workout preferences, ensuring a complete fitness experience.

  • Treadmills
  • Elliptical Trainers
  • Stationary Bikes
  • Weight Training Machines
  • Free Weights & Benches
  • Yoga Mats & Exercise Balls

Gym Rules & Timings

To ensure a safe and productive environment for everyone, please adhere to the following rules:

  • Operating Hours: 5:00 AM - 11:00 PM daily.
  • Wear appropriate athletic attire and footwear.
  • Wipe down equipment after use.
  • Return all weights and equipment to their designated places.
  • No food or open beverages are allowed in the gym area.
  • Report any damaged equipment to the hostel staff immediately.
  • Personal trainers are available upon request (additional charges may apply).

*Your safety is our priority. Please consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new exercise program.
